🛂 Australian Tourist Visa (Subclass 600)

Vietnamese passport holders must apply for a Visitor Visa (subclass 600, Tourist stream) to enter Australia. Apply online via ImmiAccount — no stamp-on-passport issued; visa is electronic and linked to the passport.

⏰ Start 10–12 weeks before departure. Processing can take 4–8+ weeks in peak season. Apply no later than late April 2026 for a safety margin.

Documents checklist

🟢 For all 4 applicants
  • Passport valid ≥ 6 months beyond return date — scanned bio page
  • Recent passport-style digital photo (white background, 45x35mm)
  • Completed Form 1419 (Application for Visitor Visa) — generated automatically online
  • Signed & translated Form 54 if children are on separate applications
  • Round-trip flight itinerary (Vietnam Airlines — booking reference is sufficient; no need to pay yet — use hold/reserve)
  • Hotel bookings for entire stay (print confirmations from Booking.com/hotel)
  • Day-by-day travel itinerary (this file exported as PDF works)
🟢 Financial evidence (Thai as primary sponsor)
  • Last 6 months personal bank statements (certified translation)
  • Salary confirmation letter from employer (in English or certified translation) — include position, tenure, salary, approved leave dates
  • Business registration + tax records if self-employed
  • Property ownership certificates (Sổ đỏ) as ties to Vietnam
  • Evidence of funds — suggest showing AUD 10,000 (VND 162M)+ balance for a family of 4 over 13 days
🟢 For the children (9 & 11 years)
  • Birth certificates with certified English translation
  • School letter confirming enrollment + approved absence dates
  • Consent letter from both parents if traveling; if both parents accompany, just include the family composition letter
  • FLL invitation / registration confirmation for your son — strong supporting document! Print it.
🟢 Supporting documents (strongly recommended)
  • Cover letter explaining purpose of trip (son attending FLL tournament) — 1 page
  • FLL event invitation or registration confirmation
  • Marriage certificate (certified translation)
  • Previous travel history (old passport stamps or visa copies — Japan, Korea, Europe all help)
  • Travel insurance for entire family (recommended Bảo Việt, PTI, AIG Travel Guard or international provider)

🚫 Should you rent a car?

Not recommended for this trip. Australia drives on the left, Sydney CBD parking is AUD 80 (VND 1.3M)+/day, and both your major day trips (Great Ocean Road, Phillip Island) are better done on guided tours where you can sleep on the bus and not worry about kangaroos at dusk. Uber + day tours saves ~AUD 400-600 (VND 6.5M-9.7M) vs rental + parking + fuel.
